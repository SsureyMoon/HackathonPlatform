Hack the world. Be borderless.

1
 Local entrepreneurs or engineers upload some issues they have confronted while operating business.
2
 After checking the issues, we contact some sponsor, and throw a hackathon to solve the issues.
3
 Hackers join the hackathon and submit projects with technical skills or creative ideas.
4
 During the hackathon, hackers can communicates with the issue raisers (local entrepreneurs or engineers) to get a better understanding, and to find more practical solution.
5
 The issue raisers vote on the project which fits to their situations.
6
 Anyone may vote on projects which they like, and share them thorough SNS. This action gathers much attention from the public.
People can also invest some money to the issue. The money invested will be used to provide airline tickets to winners.
7
 The winners get prizes, they will get a chance to work with the local entrepreneurs for a couple of months by flying to the countries.
8
 People who invest money get periodic reports, and even small benefit.
